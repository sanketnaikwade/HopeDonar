We Offer Different Services To Improve Your Health

Plasma checkup

  • Plasma is a component of blood that can be donated separately from whole blood.
  • Plasma donation typically involves apheresis, a process where blood is drawn.
  • plasma is separated, and the remaining components are returned to the donor.

WBC & RBC checkup

Donors undergo pre-donation health screening, including a check on WBC counts, to ensure their immune system is healthy.

Donors are assessed to ensure their RBC counts fall within an acceptable range, and eligibility criteria may vary based on factors such as age and gender.

Blood Transfusion

Blood transfusion is the process of transferring blood or blood components from one individual (donor) to another (recipient).

Transfusions may involve whole blood or specific blood components such as red blood cells (RBCs), plasma, platelets, or clotting factors, depending on the patient's needs.
